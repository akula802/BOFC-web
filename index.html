About us




Who we are

Bell Ornithopters Flying Club, Inc. was established in September 1974 as a perk for employees of Western Electric and Mountain Bell. Later that same year, it was expanded to include all Bell System employees.

After the breakup of Bell System in 1983, the club continued as a private organization. Over 40 years later, the club is open to the public and forever committed to safe and affordable aviation.
